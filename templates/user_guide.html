User Guide

Follow these steps to maximize your productivity using Spendora.

1. Dashboard Limits & Alerts

Your dashboard automatically checks if your expenditures bypass 80% of your earnings. Keep an eye out for warning banners at the top of your screen.

2. Voice Inputs

Use the voice commands in the Add Expense, Income, or Saving pages. Click the recording mic, tap to speak, and let Gemini interpret the item and price directly. Be as descriptive as you like!

3. Bill Scanner Integration

Snap pictures of your physical receipts or upload screenshots. The AI breaks down total billing amounts alongside the correct shop names, auto-filling your expenditure form instantly.

4. Analytical Reports

Visit Reports to view graphical representations of your tracking data, trend lines, and CSV exports for external use.
