How Family Group Works

The Family Group feature allows you and your family members to combine financial insights, providing a complete picture of your household wealth.

Features included:

Aggregated Dashboard

Switch your dashboard view to "Family" to instantly sum up the incomes, expenses, and savings of all grouped accounts.

Private Records

Though totals are combined, individual transactions remain tied to user accounts, ensuring personal privacy.

Budget Cooperation

Work against shared limits. Receive notifications when the joint spending nears 80% of aggregate monthly income.

Easy Setup

A family admin can invite members through their email ID. Once accepted, they instantly fall under the umbrella group.
